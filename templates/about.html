About

This game/interactive animation was created as a Creative Expressions Assignment in response to the book The Spirit Catches You and You Fall Down by Anne Fadiman at Stony Brook Univeristy. The novel talks about many traditions and beliefs of the Hmong people. One of the beliefs which I found particularly interesting was that spirits of people return to their placenta once they pass away. This ended up being the main point of my game - to get a soul back to it's placenta.
